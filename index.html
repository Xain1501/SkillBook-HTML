Welcome To Skill-Book

" From curious to confident — one skill at a time. "

SkillBook helps you track every skill you're learning — from programming to gardening. It's perfect for self-learners, students, and lifelong learners. Visualize your growth, stay accountable, and celebrate your progress.

What can you do with SkillBook

  • 📋 Add and organize your skills

    Quickly log any skill you're working on — from web development to cooking. Capture the name, description, and category for organized tracking.

  • 🚦 Track your learning status

    Label each skill as Pending, In Progress, or Achieved. Visually monitor how far you’ve come and what still needs work.

  • 📝 Skill Notes

    Attach short notes or learning goals to each skill. Use this space to track resources, ideas, or next steps.

  • 📊 Skill Overview Table

    View all your skills in a clean, sortable table. See descriptions, status, and update options at a glance.

  • 📆 Last Updated Timestamp

    Track when each skill was last edited. Helps you revisit skills you haven’t touched in a while.

  • 🔍 Search or Filter Skills

    Find specific skills quickly as your list grows. Filter by category, status, or keyword.

  • 🎯 Skill Categories / Tags

    Group skills into types: Tech, Creative, Physical, etc. Makes browsing and reviewing easier and more structured.

  • 🔔 Remind Yourself to Update

    Get gentle reminders to revisit stalled skills. Keeps you engaged and prevents forgotten goals.
